Some Info June 24th, 2001
"OMG! Another Space strategy!" Well, we know that there are quite a lot of these games out there. But wait!
Space Regents is going to be the next generation of those games (what did you expect? ;)
First of all. It's not copied. Sure there will be colonization, alliances and all the stuff a space strategy MUST contain.

Heres a short list of things already included:
- Big, dynamic starmap
- Systemmaps
- 10 different planettypes
- Many techs
- Many planetar & orbital buildings
- Alliances
- Travelroutes
- Jumpgates
- Trade & Traderoutes
- Pirates
- Battlefields in space and on ground
- Huge fleets and even more gigantic invasion-armys
- Covert Operations
- Nuclear, biological & chemical warfare
- Worst written english
- and more that I must have forgotten

This is going to be included in the first beta or later:
- Clean Oxford english ;)
- Admirals, with stats and inventory!
- TOP SECRET, but it'll kick ass
- Skins
- Different language support
- and more that I can't think of right now

We are going to start the first closed alpha round soon!
If you've been nice last year, did your homework and brushed your teeth you might get the chance to join the round!
Every alpha player can gain a bit of immortality by giving a constellation a name. So check back often, so you won't miss the official inofficial sign up!! :)
Runelord

First Words June 11th, 2001
Wohoo! This is the first time some info about Space Regents is posted public!

We are three people, Mop, Gorlord and me that design our little baby here. I don't really know who of us had the idea, but we were inspired by Planetarion and Imperial Conflict. The first note I found about our great plan to design Space Regents were dated around May 2000.
It said "Lass uns auch sowas wie Planetarion machen, nur besser" (Lets do something similar to Planteraion, just better ;)
I guess you will find some more info and pictures about Space Regents soon, so check back often. :)

Space Regents is going to need the new SVG Plugin (Scaleable Vector Graphics) from Adobe.
You can download the plugin at www.adobe.com/svg/.
